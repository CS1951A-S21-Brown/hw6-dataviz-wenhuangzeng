  3. List 3 reasons why D3 would not be the best tool for creating a visualization.
    • D3 has many cool and fancy visualization options which can be tempting for developer to use but those visualizations might be overcustomized and complicated for regular users to understand easily.
    • D3 requires a good amount of web development experience, which data analysts might not have. This can lead to misunderstanding between data analysts and engineer when communicating what visualization to make.
    • D3 visualizations, especially the ones with interactive features, are best viewed on a web browser on a computer. However, computer and internet might not be accessible to everyone so the amount of audiences reached can be limited.
